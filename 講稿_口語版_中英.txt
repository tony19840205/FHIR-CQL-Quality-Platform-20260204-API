口頭 1 分鐘程式介紹（邊操作版）

【中文版（約 60 秒）】

大家好，我是 Charles，FHIR-CQL Quality Platform 的創辦人。

各位有沒有想過：
我們的手機可以看到即時天氣、即時路況、各種即時資訊，
但醫療資訊，為什麼常常做不到「即時」？

舉個例子，身為家長，我想知道家附近現在的傳染病狀況，才能提早幫孩子做好防護，
可是目前很難有平台提供即時、可行動的資料。

再舉一個例子，身為醫院 CEO，想快速掌握院內整體品質與風險數據，
也不容易直接在手機上看到即時資訊。

這正是我們軟體要解決的痛點。

我們的流程很簡單：
先在醫院承辦人員的電腦部署這套系統，完成院端授權與 FHIR Server 認證；
接著啟動客製化 CQL 規則，幾分鐘內就能自動跑出完整結果。
這些數據全部來自 FHIR 標準化醫療資料的即時分析。

更方便的是，數據出來後只要按一個按鈕，
就能把結果安全地傳到後端，分享給民眾、醫院管理者，以及政府決策單位。

我們相信，醫療數據也應該像天氣預報一樣即時、透明、可行動。
謝謝大家。


【English Version (about 60 seconds, live demo style)】

Hello everyone, I’m Charles, founder of the FHIR-CQL Quality Platform.

Have you ever noticed this?
On our phones, we can get real-time weather, traffic, and all kinds of live information.
But when it comes to healthcare data, “real-time” is still rare.

For example, as a parent, I want to know the current infectious disease situation around my neighborhood, so I can better protect my child.
But today, it is hard to find a platform that provides truly real-time, actionable data.

Another example: as a hospital CEO, if you want a quick view of hospital-wide quality and risk metrics,
it is still not easy to get that information instantly on your phone.

That is exactly the problem our software solves.

Our workflow is simple:
we deploy the system on the hospital administrator’s computer, complete hospital authorization and FHIR server authentication,
then launch customized CQL rules. Within minutes, the full results are generated automatically.
All outputs come from real-time analysis of standardized FHIR data.

Even better, once results are ready, one click is enough
to securely send them to the backend and share them with citizens, hospital leaders, and government decision-makers.

We believe healthcare data should be as real-time, transparent, and actionable as a weather forecast.
Thank you.
