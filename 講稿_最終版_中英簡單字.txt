【中文版】

大家好，我是 Charles，FHIR-CQL Quality Platform 的創辦人。

各位有沒有想過：
我們的手機可以看到即時天氣、即時路況、各種即時資訊，
但醫療資訊，為什麼常常做不到「即時」？

舉個例子，身為家長，我想知道家附近現在的傳染病狀況，才能提早幫孩子做好防護，可是目前很難找到。

再舉一個例子，身為醫院 CEO，想快速掌握院內整體品質與風險數據，也不容易直接在手機上看到即時資訊。

這正是我們軟體要解決的"痛點"。

我們的流程很簡單：

首先：
在醫院承辦人員的電腦部署這套系統，完成院端授權與 FHIR Server 認證；

接著：
啟動客製化 CQL，去 FHIR 伺服器分析資料，幾分鐘內就能自動跑出完整結果。

最後：
這些數字經過後端整理，就可分享給民眾、醫院管理者，以及政府決策單位。

我相信，不久的將來，醫療數據可像天氣預報一樣即時、透明。

謝謝大家。


【English Version - Simple Words】

Hello everyone. I'm Charles, founder of the FHIR-CQL Quality Platform.

Have you ever thought about this:
Our phones can show us real-time weather, real-time traffic, and all kinds of live information.
But healthcare data - why is it so hard to get it in real-time?

Let me give you an example. As a parent, I want to know right now if there are sick people near my home, so I can protect my child.
But today, this information is very hard to find.

Another example: as a hospital leader, I want to know quickly how well my hospital is doing - the data about quality and risk.
But it's not easy to see this on my phone right now.

This is exactly the problem our software solves.

Our process is simple:

Step one:
We put this system on the hospital office computer. The hospital approves it and connects it to the FHIR Server.

Step two:
We start the CQL rules, get the data from FHIR, and in just a few minutes, we get all the results.

Step three:
The numbers come through the system, and we can share them with people, hospital leaders, and the government.

I believe that very soon, health data will be as easy to see as a weather report - fast and clear.

Thank you.
